Audience Profile

The intended audience for my work varies from those that have extensive knowledge of owning and operating a web page to those that have very little. My intent is to draw in those that don't know where to start when it comes to building their site with affordable pricing and an open an honest attitude. I would also like to make myself avalible to those that simply need someone to efficiently manage their preexisting site. Roughly the demographic for my clientele would be 20s to 50s both ruel and citywide who need help with any and everything pertaining to their website!
